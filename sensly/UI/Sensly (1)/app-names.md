# App Name Options for Sensory Monitoring System

## Evaluation Criteria
- ✅ **Memorable**: Short, easy to say and spell
- ✅ **Meaningful**: Conveys purpose (early warning, protection, calm)
- ✅ **Emotional resonance**: Parents feel hope, not clinical/cold
- ✅ **Not deficit-focused**: Empowering, not about "fixing" autism
- ✅ **Brandable**: .com/.app available, works globally
- ✅ **Sayable**: Works in conversation ("I use Sentinel")

---

## TIER 1: Top Recommendations ⭐⭐⭐

### 1. **Aurora**
**Meaning**: Early morning light; warning before sunrise; natural phenomenon of beauty

**Why it works**:
- 🌅 "Light before the dawn" = early warning system
- Beautiful, hopeful imagery (not clinical)
- Nature metaphor (autism community embraces neurodiversity)
- **Tagline**: "See the light before the storm"
- Short, memorable, works globally

**Positioning**: "Aurora helps you see what's coming, so you can guide your child to calm."

---

### 2. **Pulse**
**Meaning**: Heartbeat; life rhythm; the pulse of wellbeing

**Why it works**:
- 💓 Medical/health connection (monitoring vitals)
- Rhythmic, constant (always watching)
- Action-oriented (take the pulse of the situation)
- **Tagline**: "Feel the rhythm of regulation"
- Modern, tech-forward sound

**Positioning**: "Pulse monitors the heartbeat of your child's sensory world."

---

### 3. **Haven**
**Meaning**: Safe place; shelter from storm; sanctuary

**Why it works**:
- 🏠 Emotional safety (what every parent wants)
- Protective without being fearful
- Creates mental image of calm space
- **Tagline**: "Your safe space in the sensory storm"
- Warm, accessible, parent-friendly

**Positioning**: "Haven is the safe space between overwhelm and calm."

---

### 4. **Beacon**
**Meaning**: Signal light; warning system; guide in darkness

**Why it works**:
- 🔦 Navigation/guidance metaphor
- Early warning connotation (lighthouse warns ships)
- Light in darkness (hopeful)
- **Tagline**: "Navigate the sensory world together"
- Strong, trustworthy sound

**Positioning**: "Beacon guides you through the unpredictable sensory landscape."

---

### 5. **Anchor**
**Meaning**: Stability; grounding; security in chaos

**Why it works**:
- ⚓ Grounding metaphor (sensory grounding is a real intervention)
- Stability when everything feels chaotic
- Nautical theme (calm waters vs storms)
- **Tagline**: "Stay grounded when the world feels overwhelming"
- Solid, dependable feeling

**Positioning**: "Anchor keeps you grounded in the sensory storm."

### 10. **Echo**
