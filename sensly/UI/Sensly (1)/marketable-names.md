## Modern Naming Trends Analysis

**What's working in 2025**:
- ✅ Short (5-7 letters)
- ✅ .app or .io domains (not .com obsession)
- ✅ Suffixes: -ly, -ify, -able
- ✅ Modified spellings (vowel removal, unique Q/X)
- ✅ Compound words that create new meaning

**What's outdated**:
- ❌ "The [Name]" format
- ❌ Long descriptive names
- ❌ Generic words (Calm, Focus, etc.)
- ❌ Must-have .com at any cost
